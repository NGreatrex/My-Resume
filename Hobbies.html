My Hobbies and interests

  • Music

    I enjoy playing music in my spare time in my band Bad Heritage, in that band I play Drums but I also play quite a few different instruments.

    To name a few in order of level of skill

    1. Drums
    2. Banjo
    3. Ukulele
    4. Guitar
    5. Bass
    6. Piano

  • Gaming

    When I'm not behind a drum kit I like to play a variety of different video games, from strategic team based games, to deeply narrative rich roleplaying games (a counter product of what you will soon read below).

  • Table-top roleplaying games

    I often like to arrange games nights, at my house and invite a group to participate in a pre-planned adventure where I as the games master will guide them through a tale where they can all work together to achieve a common goal. This can very often go of the pre-planned track and I will have improvise methods of getting a large group back onto track with their mission.
